Protein Breakfast Brownie

Description

Protein flavored baked brownie recipe. You can have it as either a breakfast meal or a snack! Makes 6 portions in ~40mins total.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 30g vanilla protein
  • 60g protein flavor of your choosing
  • 120g steel cut oats
  • 120g old fashioned oats
  • 4g baking powder
  • 20g cocoa powder
  • 56g chocolate chips

Wet Ingredients

  • 150g egg whites or 2 large eggs
  • 42g maple syrup
  • 480g milk
  • 200g apple sauce
  • 14g butter
