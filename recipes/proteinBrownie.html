Protein Breakfast Brownie

Description

Protein flavored baked brownie recipe. You can have it as either a breakfast meal or a snack! Makes 6 portions in ~45mins total.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 30g vanilla protein
  • 60g protein flavor of your choosing
  • 120g steel cut oats
  • 120g old fashioned oats
  • 4g baking powder
  • 20g cocoa powder
  • 56g chocolate chips

Wet Ingredients

  • 150g egg whites or 2 large eggs
  • 42g maple syrup
  • 480g milk
  • 200g apple sauce
  • 14g butter

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mix dry ingredients except the chocolate chips in a large bowl
  3. Add in wet ingredients and mix
  4. Get a deep pan and spray it with a oil or baking spray
  5. Add in the protein brownie mix and top off with chocolate chips
  6. Bake in oven for 35mins
  7. Let rest for 10mins then cut into 6 equal pieces
