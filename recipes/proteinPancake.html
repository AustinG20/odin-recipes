Protein Pancakes

Description

Protein pancakes made with oat flour, incorporating both casein and whey protein. Makes 4 pancakes in ~15mins

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 12.5g vanilla casein protein
  • 12.5g vanilla whey
  • 20g oat flour
  • 10g tapioca flour or cornstarch
  • 1g baking powder

Wet Ingredients

  • 60g egg whites
  • 75g plain nonfat greek yogurt
  • 25g water

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat skillet on medium heat
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Add in wet ingredients and mix
  4. Spray skillet with oil
  5. Add batter to skillet and flip after a couple of minutes
